Problem Statement

Youth in Rwanda face numerous challenges in finding
employment, including a lack of access to job opportunities,
inadequate skills development, and limited mentorship and guidance.
This results in high youth unemployment rates, hindering their economic
participation and limiting their potential to contribute to Rwanda's development.

Proposed Solution

Empowering Youth to Ascend is a comprehensive youth employment
platform that provides personalized job matching, skill development
training, community support, and real-time guidance to help Rwandan
youth find fulfilling employment and contribute to their country's sustainable
development. It caters to the unique needs of Rwandan youth by offering a mobile-first
design, multilingual support, and culturally sensitive content to ensure inclusivity and accessibility for all youth.
